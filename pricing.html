FAQ
Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

Click any "Book Now" button on the site, fill out the quick inquiry form, and we'll be in touch within 24 hours to confirm details, answer questions, and lock in your date. A 50% deposit secures your booking — the balance is due on delivery of your gallery.
Portrait and headshot sessions run 1–2 hours. Commercial and brand sessions are typically booked as half-day (4 hours) or full-day (8 hours) rates. Event and wedding coverage ranges from 4 to 12+ hours. Post-production and editing projects are delivered within 3–5 business days.
Portrait and headshot galleries are delivered within 3 business days. Commercial and brand sessions within 5 business days. Event and wedding galleries within 5 business days. Rush turnaround (24–48 hours) is available for most services — just ask when booking.
For personal sessions you receive a full personal-use license — you can share, print, post, and display your photos freely. Commercial sessions include a commercial-use license covering advertising, websites, social media, and marketing materials. All licenses are perpetual with no expiration date.
Rescheduling is free with at least 48 hours' notice — just contact us and we'll find a new date. Cancellations more than 72 hours before the session are fully refundable minus a 5% processing fee. Cancellations within 72 hours may forfeit the deposit, but we're always happy to work with you if something unexpected comes up.
Absolutely. We shoot commercial projects, destination weddings, and events nationwide. Travel is billed at cost (flights, hotel, ground transport) and discussed transparently during booking. Contact us early for out-of-state work to check availability and plan logistics.
We shoot on professional Canon and Sony mirrorless systems with a full complement of prime and zoom lenses. Lighting uses Profoto strobes and LED continuous setups. Video is captured in 4K RAW and graded in DaVinci Resolve. Editing is done in Lightroom, Photoshop, and DaVinci Resolve.
After your session you'll receive login credentials to your private client portal. Inside you can view your gallery, download full-resolution files once payment is complete, approve selects, communicate directly with the JWM team, and track your order status.
Session payments are handled through your client portal and processed securely via Stripe. We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Wire transfer is available for larger commercial projects — contact us for details.
